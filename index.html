Decide to Donate

Donating to support organizations that address covid challenges while we are in the midst of the crisis is fraught: friends send appeals; we notice celebrity endorsements. All of these appeals seem urgent and essential, further complicating our decision. What would be helpful is to understand the impact of our donation. This website aims to help you create a portfolio of charitable work organizations, understand the impact of your charity portfolio on covid response in India, and then make a donation.
